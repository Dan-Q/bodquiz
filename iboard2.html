Bodleian Christmas Quiz

How To Play

You will need:

  • Your phone or tablet
  • A WiFi login

How to play…

  • Connect to Eduroam or Bodleian Libraries WiFi
  • Go to www.bodleian.ox.ac.uk/quiz (or scan the QR code below)
  • Enter your name at the bottom (or you'll be given a pseudonym!)
  • Start playing along

You have 30 seconds to answer each question.

Answers and scoreboards are shown on the big screen.
